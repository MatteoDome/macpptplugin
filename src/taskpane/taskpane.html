Help & Information

About This Add-in

The PowerPoint Shortcuts add-in provides quick access to professional formatting, elements, and productivity tools. Use keyboard shortcuts or the buttons in this panel to enhance your PowerPoint workflow.

Limitations

Due to Office Add-in restrictions, some shortcuts cannot be implemented:

  • Ctrl+1, Ctrl+2, Ctrl+6, Ctrl+0 (single digit keys)
  • Alt+Q (missing second modifier)
  • Function key combinations (Fn+F2, etc.)
  • Ctrl+T (browser shortcut conflict)

Alternative Solutions

For unsupported shortcuts, consider using:

  • Windows: AutoHotkey for custom shortcuts
  • Mac: Keyboard Maestro for automation
  • All platforms: Use the buttons in this panel
